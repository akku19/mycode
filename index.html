Features

Make Videos: In this feature users can make videos by recording the video and adding sound from the audio files that are available in the app. After creating the video, the user can then share it instantly on the platform and other social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram etc.

Editing Videos: In this features users can edit their videos by changing the color of the eyes, hair, and even adding makeup. There are also many stickers, animations, and masks.

Eye Catching Homepage: This feature helps the users to view all the videos related to their personal interests quickly. Depending upon the interests mentioned by the user when logging into the app, the corresponding videos that are shared by other users will be shown to that particular user on the homepage of the app. The user can just swipe up to see the list of all related videos and can swipe right to view the profile details of the user who shared that video.

Live streaming and Gifts. In this TikTok feature, video creators can make livestreams and receive virtual goods from their fans purchased with TikTok Coins.

Premium Users: Our Tiktok clone allows the premium users to upload audio and video files of their own personal choice without any hassle to the video-sharing platform. After uploading files, the user can then proceed to edit and share those videos on the platform

Followers/Following: With this feature of our Tiktok clone, the user of the app can view the list of users who are all following them on the platform.

Duet Option: This feature will be loved by users who like to sing. TikTokers can create a video, add a #duetwithme hashtag, and ask other users to duet with them.
